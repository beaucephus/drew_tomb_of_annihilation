====== Staff of Healing ======
Created Wednesday 01 July 2020

This staff has 10 Charges.
While holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 or more of its Charges to cast one of the following Spells from it, using your spell save DC and Spellcasting Ability modifier: 
Cure Wounds (1 charge per Spell Level, up to 4th)
Lesser Restoration (2 charges)
Mass Cure Wounds (5 charges).

The staff regains 1d6 + 4 expended Charges daily at dawn. 
If you expend the last charge, roll a d20. 
On a 1. the staff vanishes in a flash of light, lost forever.
