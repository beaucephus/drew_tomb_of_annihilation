====== Sword of the Fisha ======
Created Tuesday 05 November 2019

This sword appears and functions as an ordinary short sword when in its inactive state. You may activate this sword to transform it into a magnificent magical short sword and make a single weapon attack with it, ending the transformation. This attack is made with advantage and deals an extra 3d6 radiant damage. This transformation has 3 charges that reset daily at dawn.
